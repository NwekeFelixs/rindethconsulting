About Us

Rindeth Consulting Limited is a registered business consulting firm with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) of Nigeria, specializing in the development and scaling of tech startups, business structuring, process optimization, branding, and fund acquisition. With a strong focus on providing support to technology-driven enterprises, the firm has consistently worked to foster innovation, entrepreneurship, and economic development in Nigeria. Rindeth Consulting is well-positioned to execute the activities and initiatives proposed.

Our Mission

To drive the development of tech-based solutions in Nigeria by empowering startups and young innovators through business development services, mentorship, and strategic partnerships.

Our Vision

To become a leading catalyst for technological innovation and entrepreneurial growth in Africa, with a focus on fostering sustainable development through tech-based solutions.

Our Approach

We understand that every business is unique, which is why our approach is personalized and collaborative. We take the time to deeply understand your business, its challenges, and its goals. From there, we develop customized strategies and action plans designed to deliver tangible results and align with your vision.
