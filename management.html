Meet Our Team

Amaechi Prince Chekwube

CEO/Lead Consultant

Prince Amaechi is a visionary business strategist, tech innovator, and entrepreneur dedicated to driving sustainable growth, economic development, and business excellence. With over a decade of hands-on experience in business development, strategic planning, financial management, and enterprise capacity building, he has become a key player in Nigeria’s entrepreneurial ecosystem.

As the Founder and CEO of Rindeth Consulting Limited, he leads a dynamic team committed to equipping businesses with the tools, strategies, and innovation needed to scale, attract investment, and thrive in competitive markets.

Through his expertise, he has trained over 3,000 MSMEs, mentored more than 100 SMEs and startups, and played a crucial role in strengthening Nigeria’s business landscape by making enterprises investment-ready, increasing revenue, and generating employment. He is among the first 20 Enterprise Support Organizations (ESOs) trained on the Argidius Scale Framework in Nigeria, reinforcing his leadership in impact-driven business development.

His mission is to empower entrepreneurs, CEOs, and organizations with cutting-edge business strategies, digital transformation solutions, and financial management systems that drive profitability and long-term sustainability. As a tech innovator, he is passionate about leveraging technology to streamline business processes, optimize efficiency, and enhance market competitiveness for SMEs and startups. His work is guided by core values of integrity, excellence, innovation, and collaboration, ensuring that every intervention translates into measurable growth and socio-economic impact.

Amaechi holds a degree in Accounting from Abia State University, Uturu, and an MBA in Business Management from Tekedia Institute. His certifications include Certified Grant Writer (School of Grant Success, Lagos), Associate Accounting Technician (AAT, ABWA), Entrepreneurship Development (SEEDC), Embedded Enterprise Education (Pan Atlantic University, Lagos), Business Development Service Provider (BDSP, Kaduna Business School), and Argidius Scale Framework (Impact Investors Foundation).

Recognized as a TEF Mentor, BDSP for Arya Women Nigeria, and Consultant with the Enterprise Development Center (EDC), Pan Atlantic University, Amaechi works closely with investors, government agencies, and international organizations to design and implement high-impact business solutions. His overarching vision is to create a thriving entrepreneurial ecosystem where businesses are investment-ready, revenue-generating, and employment-driven—fostering economic resilience and sustainable development across Africa.
